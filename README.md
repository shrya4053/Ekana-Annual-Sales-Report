# Ekana-Annual-Sales-Report
This project analyzes past sales data of Ekana Clothing Store to evaluate overall performance, customer demand, and product trends. The goal is to identify what sells best, where improvements are needed, and which cities show the highest potential for expansion.

## Project Objective
To take out a detailed analysis of its historical sales and operational data from the past few years. The goal of this analysis is to derive meaningful insights about overall business performance, identify areas that require improvement, and understand product-wise and region-wise demand trends. Based on these insights, the store aims to strategically expand into new cities where there is high demand and growth potential.

## Questions
- Which month recorded the highest sales and order count, and how does it compare to the lowest month?
- Which gender contributes more to total sales, and what percentage of revenue comes from women vs men?
- What is the dominant order status, and how do cancelled, returned, and refunded orders compare to delivered orders?
- Which state has the highest sales among the top five, and how do Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh differ in performance?
- Which age group contributes the most to sales, and in which age group do women dominate over men?
- Which channel generates the highest revenue, and how does Amazon compare to Flipkart and Myntra?
- Which sales channel has the lowest contribution to overall performance?
- How do sales trends vary month-wise when filtering by channel or category?

## Process
- Verified data for any missing values and anomalities, and sort out the same.
- Made sure data is consisted and clean with respect to data type, data format and values used.
- Grouped the required data to categorize it.
- Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.
- Merged all pivot tables into one dashboard and applied slicer to make it dynamic.
